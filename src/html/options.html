Voice DIM Options

Always Listening Mode

Always listening mode requires an activation word/phrase in order for Voice DIM to perform a command. It's recommended to use at least 2 syllables (3 preferred) for your activation word/phrase for better performance.

When using your activation phrase, you don't need an extended pause between the phrase and your command. It's not like other "magic word" services where you wait for a prompt. Just say "<activation phrase> <command>", no pause.

Always listening mode

Note: If you include "dim" in your phrase, it is often interpreted as "them" by speech recognition, so I don't recommend including it. That being said, there's a mitigation for it in the code if you do.
